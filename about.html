About Us

Shaping Global Citizens with Noble Hearts and Noble Minds

Nobel International School is a premier educational institution that provides a complete educational journey for students from Early Years (Ages 4 to 6), Elementary (Year 1 to 6), Junior and Senior School (Year 7 to 11). The school is dedicated to providing students with a well-rounded education that goes beyond the classroom, and it is committed to fostering a love of learning in every student, along with transforming our students with noble characters and compassion to reach out to the underprivileged community.

Wholesome education where a child is given opportunities to reach his/her full potential is the heartbeat of Nobel’s founder, Ms Clarine Chun. Thus, the school’s programmes are aimed to optimise the learning experience of every child. Nobel International School was founded in 2004 by a group of educators and business leaders who saw the need for a high-quality school in the city. They believed that students should be exposed to a diverse and challenging curriculum that would prepare them for success in the global community.

Ms Clarine Chun, who is also the founder of the FunGates System for preschools, established Nobel to carry through what was started in the kindergartens. FunGates is designed with a strong emphasis on building integrity and values as part of the character development of a child, thereby producing children who perform better academically, socially and spiritually. Nobel offers a continuation of that programme to help build a solid foundation in a child’s life, enabling them to grow intellectually, physically, spiritually, and morally.

Our Mission

To Develop this Institution into a Temple of Learning by Including Ethical, Moral and Social Values in All Disciplines of Study and by Providing Quality Education and Opportunities for All Round Development of the Students with Sterling Characters.

Our Vision

To engrave character and Value Based Education to Increase the Strength of Mind in Young Children and Youth to Manifest the Perfection already in Man.
